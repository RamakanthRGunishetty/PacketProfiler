OAM

Operations, Administration and Maintenance is traffic that involves network management tasks such as fault detection, performance monitoring, and configuration management.It ensures the network operates efficiently and helps in maintaining and troubleshooting the network infrastructure.

VoIP

Voice over Internet Protocol is a technology that allows voice communication and multimedia sessions over the Internet. It converts voice signals into digital data packets and transmits them over the network, enabling phone calls over the internet.

Bulk

Bulk data traffic consists of large volumes of data transferred over the network, often for purposes like backups, data replication, or software updates. This type of traffic can be bandwidth-intensive and may be scheduled during off-peak hours to avoid network congestion

Control

Control traffic is used for signaling and managing the flow of data within the network. It includes messages that coordinate the establishment, maintenance, and termination of data connections, ensuring proper network functionality and quality of service.

Critical

Critical traffic includes data that is essential for the operation of critical systems, such as healthcare or financial transactions. This type of traffic requires high priority and low latency to ensure timely and reliable delivery.

Default

Default traffic refers to standard or typical network traffic that does not require special handling or prioritization. It represents general-purpose data communication without specific quality of service requirements.

P2P

Peer-to-Peer traffic involves direct data exchange between devices on the network without requiring a central server. This type of traffic is commonly used for file sharing and decentralized applications, often leading to significant bandwidth usage.

Signaling

Signaling traffic is used to establish, control, and terminate connections within a network. It includes protocols and messages that manage call setups, routing, and other network control functions, essential for telecommunications and internet services.

Transaction

Transactional data involves small, discrete packets of information that represent individual transactions or exchanges of data. This type of traffic is common in financial services, e-commerce, and online applications where quick, reliable exchanges are crucial.

Video

Video streaming traffic involves the continuous transmission of video content over the network, allowing users to watch videos in real-time without downloading them. This type of traffic requires high bandwidth and low latency to ensure smooth playback and high video quality.
